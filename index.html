Projects.exe

Envelope - Internet-Connected Message Display

This device is placed on a desk or nightstand, displaying real-time messages sent over the internet in a stylized package. Built using a $20 Cheap-Yellow-Display with an ESP32.

3D Space Mapping LiDAR Scanner

A comprehensive LiDAR scanner designed and constructed entirely from the ground up. Built with a TOF sensor, 2 motors, and a microcontroller. Programmed in C++ and Python.

AggTea - Shop Management System

A robust, web interface modeled after a boba tea store, providing a customer kiosk, cashier point-of-sale system, manager analytics and menu board. Created with Next.JS, React, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, and Tailwind CSS.

ChatGPT/Notion Study Guide Generator

A full-stack Gantt Chart generator that feeds study topics directly into a user's Notion account via the Notion API. Built for the 2025 Build4Good Hackathon.

Arduino LCD Menu Screen

A C++ based menu screen for an LCD using a rotary encoder, designed for Arduino components.

Peer Mentor/TA for FYEX 101 - Hullabaloo U, Texas A&M

August 2024 - December 2024

In my Sophomore year, I had the pleasure of working as a TA for an introductory course called Hullabaloo U at A&M. I got to work with a great instructor who provided me with opportunities for independence and support when needed. As a peer mentor, I helped students navigate academic challenges by presenting lectures and leading activities alongside my instructor. We covered a wide range of topics covering ideas from study strategies to relationships and mental health. Furthermore, throughout the semester, I held one-on-ones with every student, getting to know them personally and hear about their goals. Although I haven't had the time to teach more sections of this class, I did really enjoy the semester.
